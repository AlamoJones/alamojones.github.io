ABOUT BERNIE’S

We started Bernie’s to share our passion for fresh, vibrant flavors and to create a treat that evokes the joy of summer in every bite. Inspired by our love for farm-fresh ingredients and nostalgic moments, we wanted to craft a granita that is both indulgent and wholesome.

By using all-natural ingredients and artisanal techniques, we aim to deliver a refreshing and luxurious experience that connects people to the beauty of nature. Our hope is that each cup sparks a memory or creates a new moment of bliss, reminding us all to savor life’s simple pleasures.

So what exactly is a granita? A Granita is a dessert that’s made by freezing fresh juices and purees. Once frozen, the mixture is finely shaved to achieve a light, soft texture that’s melt-in-your-mouth smooth and refreshingly airy all at once. 

And we don’t stop there! All of our granitas are finished with a campfire marshmallow topping that adds an airy, decadent texture that’ll keep you coming back for more.

Think farm stand quality sorbet meets a snow cone. But we aren’t a snow cone at all. At Bernie’s, you won’t find any artificial flavors, chemical sweeteners or sticky syrups pooled on top of frozen water. Here’s what you will find: farm-fresh ingredients, all-natural cane sugar, and clean, honest flavors.
